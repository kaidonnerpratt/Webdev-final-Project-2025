I’m Chris Stegh—a tech strategist, Microsoft MVP, and someone who’s been helping organizations turn technology into a competitive edge for over 25 years. I currently serve as CTO + VP of Strategy at eGroup Enabling Technologies, where I get to work with amazing teams and clients to build smarter, more secure, and AI-powered workplaces.

My journey started in enterprise IT, moved through leading emerging tech at a Fortune 1000 company, and eventually brought me into the consulting world. Since 2007, I’ve been helping small and mid-sized businesses make sense of complex tech and turn it into real results.

At eGroup Enabling Technologies, I focus on building strategic roadmaps around Microsoft 365 Copilot, cybersecurity, collaboration, and change management. I also write, speak, and share ideas—whether it’s through webinars, industry events, or my book, Elevate the Enterprise Using Microsoft 365 Copilot. It’s a practical guide for CxOs, IT pros, and power users who want to make AI work for them—not the other way around.

When I’m not working with clients or diving into the latest Microsoft updates, I’m probably brainstorming with fellow MVPs, writing something new, or finding ways to make strategy sessions a little more fun and a lot more impactful.
