The Enterprise

Generative AI is still in its early days, but it’s already reshaping how we work, think, and lead. We’re at a moment that feels as big as the launch of the Internet or the rise of the smartphone. Everyone’s experimenting. Organizations are trying to strike the right balance between risk and reward. And the pace? It’s relentless. That’s why he wrote Elevate the Enterprise Using Microsoft 365 Copilot. He wanted to give power users, IT leaders, and execs a practical, real-world guide to navigating this next wave of transformation—with clarity, confidence, and a little less chaos. Updated in October 2024, this book is the result of 18 months of hands-on research, testing, and working with over 200 organizations. It’s packed with insights on how to use, secure, and scale Copilot across your workplace—without losing your mind (or your data).
